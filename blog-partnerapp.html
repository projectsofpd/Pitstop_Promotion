About the app

by Shanzah on July 10, 2018
The Pitstop Partner application is a multidimensional database that makes the workforce efficient and guarantees a smooth flow in the doorstep car services offered. It consists of systematic, step by step procedures right from the inspection of the vehicle till the time the vehicle is ready to hit the road. The app ensures a continuous connectivity between the customer, the service advisor and the manager.

Objectives of the app

The app encompasses and fulfils the 5 core principles of Pitstop

CONVENIANCE-

Since written and verbal transactions are subject to discrepancies, the pitstop partner app uses technology to record and store every detail about the servicing process. This reduces time consumption and makes car servicing convenient for both the customer and the service advisor.

PRICING -
The quotation section of the app ensures fair pricing offered by pitstop and also gives the customer the freedom to either accept or reject it. The service advisor is saved from the hassle of negotiations.
TAT (Turn Around Time) –
Service advisors can access every information with just a click and this saves the time which would have otherwise been wasted in placing calls and locating service destinations.
QUALITY –
The app ensures quality in the services offered as all the complaints of the customer are dealt with. The app also gives the service advisor a chance to record issues that have not been already put forward by the customer at the time of registration.
TRANSEPARENCY –
The full body inspection and recording of the state of the vehicle during the check-in and check-out processes guarantees transparency between the customer and the service provider.





How to get started?

Login to the pitstop partner app using:

  • OTP (One Time Password) section wherein a password will be sent as a SMS to your registered mobile number.
  • Username and Password
Once you have entered the password, click on 'Login' button and viola, you have successfully logged in.

Going Online!


The Dashboard

After logging in, you will be directed to the home page of the app which is also known as the 'DASHBOARD'. It will show the complete list of active orders which are assigned to your workshop under 4 tabs - All, Pickup, Doorstep, and Self. You can Search, Sort, or Filter orders on various parameters through the toolbar on the top.

Your order will be displayed with 3 dots on the top right corner. Click on the 3 dots and select ‘Assign Owner.’ In the box ‘Select Order Owner,’ select the down arrow to assign an owner. Next, click on ‘Assign Owner.’

Type of Orders

Broadly, there are 3 type of orders - PICKUP, DOORSTEP, and SELF

PICKUP ORDER:

It is a type of order wherein a driver is required to pick up the customer vehicle and drop it to the workshop for requested servicing to be carried out. Similarly, post completion of service, a driver is arranged to drop the vehicle back to the customer.

DOORSTEP ORDER:

It is a type of order which requires a trained & certified mechanic to visit the customer's place and carry out the requested service with necessary tools and equipments.

SELF ORDER:

It is a type of order wherein a customer directly walks-in to the workshop to get the vehicle serviced.

The Check-In process!

Check in is the act of taking the custody of concerned vehicle (which needs to be serviced). Taking into custody requires noting down all the necessary details like odometer, fuel level, validating registration number, taking photographs, noting down all the issues as narrated by the customer, etc. and finally taking a hand-over sign off from the customer.

When to do check-in?

Check-In through Partner App is to be done only in case of DOORSTEP and SELF Order. Mechanic should do check-in once:
  • he has started moving from current location towards customer's location in case of DOORSTEP Order, or
  • customer has arrived at the workshop in case of SELF Order

Why should one do check-in?

Checking-in is important to have a digital record of the condition of the vehicle while taking the same under custody.

How to do check-in?

  • Click on your order and select ‘Check in.’ (Make sure that your GPS settings are enabled)
  • Start the Check-In job by swiping the button on bottom towards right, once you are about to leave for the location of your service
  • Swipe right on ‘Locate Location’ once you reach the location of your service
  • Swipe right on ‘Start Check In’ once the location is in your custody after the handover
  • Enter the ‘Registration Number’ and the ‘Odometer’ reading. Click ‘Next.’
  • In the ‘Fuel Level’ option, drag the dot towards right or left to show the approximate level of fuel in the vehicle before the servicing starts. E is for empty and F is for full.
  • Click on the small boxes present on the right hand side of the options under ‘Accessories, Mirrors, Electricals,’ to make a list of all the objects present in the vehicle in good condition. Click ‘Next.’
  • Note down the visible problems in the vehicle that have not been recorded yet. Start typing in the ‘Search Issue’ box for the issue that you wish to select or mention. Click ‘Next.’
  • You will see pictures of a vehicle exterior body from 5 different angles. This is to indicate damages to the body of the vehicle. To mark- select the pencil symbol in the top right corner, select the appropriate color from below (orange for dents, blue for scratches and red for broken parts), zoom in on the picture to mark. Click ‘Next.’
  • In the 6 boxes given, attach pictures of the vehicle from different angles. To attach image- Tap on the first box over ‘Front,’ select camera and click a picture of the front side of the vehicle. Select ‘OK’ to attach image. Click ‘Next.’
  • Take customer sign-off in the blank space provided. Click ‘Next.’
  • Swipe right on ‘Complete Job.’
Great! Check-In of the vehicle is now successfully completed.
