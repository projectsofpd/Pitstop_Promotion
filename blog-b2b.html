About the app

by Shanzah on July 25, 2018
The Pitstop Partner application is a multidimensional database that makes the workforce efficient and guarantees a smooth flow in the doorstep car services offered. It consists of systematic, step by step procedures right from the inspection of the vehicle till the time the vehicle is ready to hit the road. The app ensures a continuous connectivity between the customer, the service advisor and the manager.

Objectives of the app

The app encompasses and fulfils the 5 core principles of Pitstop

INTEGRATED CRM-

Since written and verbal transactions are subject to discrepancies, the pitstop partner app uses technology to record and store every detail about the servicing process. This reduces time consumption and makes car servicing convenient for both the customer and the service advisor.

TIME UPDATES-
The quotation section of the app ensures fair pricing offered by pitstop and also gives the customer the freedom to either accept or reject it. The service advisor is saved from the hassle of negotiations.
CONVENIENCE-
Service advisors can access every information with just a click and this saves the time which would have otherwise been wasted in placing calls and locating service destinations.
BUSINESS ANALYTICS–
The app ensures quality in the services offered as all the complaints of the customer are dealt with. The app also gives the service advisor a chance to record issues that have not been already put forward by the customer at the time of registration.
ACCESS CONTROL –
The full body inspection and recording of the state of the vehicle during the check-in and check-out processes guarantees transparency between the customer and the service provider.

1) Create a booking for an existing vehicle


  • To generate a new booking, use your login credentials to login to the B2B portal.
  • From the navigation bar on the left side of the page, select Manage Bookings.
  • From the drop down list, select New Bookings. You will be directed to the page with title New Bookings.


  • Click on +Create Booking option on the top right corner of the page. Create Booking form pops up.


  • In the Search and Select For Autofill section, fill in either of the details by typing - Registration No., Chassis No. or Mobile No.


  • As soon as either of these fields are filled, the system will show prior records it has against the field filled. From the drop down list, choose the vehicle you were searching for.
  • You will notice that the Vehicle Details and Customer Details section will get auto filled.
  • Scroll down and come to the Appointment details section.
  • Under Appointment Details section, fill in the appointment date and time using the calendar feature. Enter it correctly as the customer will be expecting the driver or technician at his location during this time.


  • Expected Delivery Date/Time is the time during which the customer will be expecting his vehicle to be delivered at his location post servicing. You can fill this using the calendar icon. But it is recommended to leave it blank at this stage.
  • In the customer notes section, enter the car issues being faced by the customer. Enter the issue by typing.
  • Under Booking Details section, enter the source from the searchable drop down menu. Source means the origin or where the booking is coming from.
  • Under Convenience Type, fill in the mode of service from the searchable drop down menu. Convenience type means the mode of service.
    • Doorstep-customer is looking for service at doorstep
    • Pickup/Drop-Customer is looking for service at garage and wants pickup and drop option
    • Self-Customer is looking for service at garage and wants to drop his vehicle himself to the garage
  • Choose the Service Type from the radio button.
  • Under Service Type, select from the options- General (like engine oil change), Maintenance (like clutch repair), Accident (damage to vehicle) or Inspection (check-up of vehicle).
  • Select create booking
  • You are directed to the Booking Details Page and a success message is displayed on top of this page.
  • A new booking will be created.

4) Cancel a booking


For some unfortunate reason, if the confirmed booking has to be cancelled, go to Change Status option in the Action Zone in the Booking Details Page.


  • A pop up will open with title Update Booking Status.
  • Choose the booking status from the searchable drop-down option
    • Enquiry-Initial Status of a booking
    • Confirmed-When the booking has to be confirmed to order
    • Cancelled-In case the booking needs to be cancelled
    • Invalid-In case the booking needs to be marked as invalid
  • Change the status to Cancelled. Select a reason for cancellation from the searchable drop down menu. Click on Update Booking Status button. Kindly note that without choosing a reason for cancellation, the booking cannot be cancelled.


  • A Success message is displayed and the booking is cancelled.
  • The Status Bar moves to the Cancelled status and the same is displayed in the Booking Timeline.

  • Pickup/Drop jobs are those jobs wherein the the service is done at Pitstop certified garage
  • Vehicle is picked up from the address provided by the customer, taken to a Pitstop a Pitstop certified garage for servicing and dropped off at the address designated by the customer
  • Also driver when picking up the vehicle performs the check in process and while dropping off the vehicle performs the checkout process through the driver app.

    C) PICKUP/DROP ORDERS



    1) What are Pickup/Drop Orders?


    Pickup/Drop orders are those orders wherein the vehicle needs to be picked up from the address provided by the customer, taken to a local garage for servicing and dropped off at the address designated by the customer.

    2) Pickup/Drop Orders Page-


    • Use your login credentials to login to the B2B portal.
    • From the navigation bar on the left side of the page, select Manage Orders.
    • From the drop down list, select Pickup/Drop Orders. You will be directed to the Pickup/Drop Orders page.


    • The Pickup/Drop Orders page has a quotation bar which shows- Pending Quote, Quote Uploaded, Pending Approval, Quote Approved.
    • You will be directed to a page with 8 columns by default, which provide information about the order.
    • Use show/hide columns to include or exclude display of columns from the drop down list. Check mark the options that you wish to see. Uncheck the options that you wish to hide.
    • Brief column description-
      1. Order ID- Unique identification no. of pickup/drop order
      2. Customer Details – Name and contact number of customer
      3. Car Details -Registration no. and Make/model of the customer vehicle
      4. Merchant- Name of the garage
      5. Appointment- Expected arrival time of driver to customer location to pick up the vehicle
      6. EDT (Estimated Delivery Time)- Time at which the vehicle needs to be dropped off at customer’s location
      7. Amount- Total cost of Quotation
      8. Status- Current state of the order
      9. Source- Origin of the order
      10. Service Type- The type of service which was booked
      11. Ongoing- time when the technician starts the service
      12. Aging- Present time minus ongoing time. It indicates a delay in service.
      13. Action- Consists of job card (issues faced by customer in the vehicle) and update (Last update in Job Timeline)
      14. Job Delay- Reached time minus appointment time. It indicates if there was a delay in reaching the customer’s location
    • Orders can be filtered by the Ongoing column (time when the service has started for the vehicle)
      • Today-Shows the list of all orders where the service started today and where it reached ongoing today
      • Yesterday-Shows the list of all orders which have been in ongoing status since yesterday
      • All-Shows the entire list of orders
    • Export button lets the user downloads the entire list of orders in an excel format.
    • All columns can be arranged chronologically (ascending or descending order) and are usually sorted by the appointment time.
    • To sort a column click on the triangle icon placed on right side of each cell. When a column is sorted the triangle icon turns blue.
    • To search for a specific order-
      • Drag your cursor to the blank box next to the Search option on the right side of the page.
      • Click on the blank box and enter the Order ID that you intend to search.
      • The details related to the search will be displayed in the list below.
    • Scroll down and click on the forward arrow to view bookings of next page or backward arrow to the previous page or click on a particular page no to land on that particular page.
